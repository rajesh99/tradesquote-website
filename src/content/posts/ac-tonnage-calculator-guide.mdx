An **ac tonnage calculator** converts square footage or BTU into tons — the standard unit for central air capacity. Searchers ask the same question different ways: **air conditioning tonnage calculator**, **air conditioner ton calculator**, **estimate ac tonnage**, **determining ac tonnage**, or **figuring ac tonnage**. One ton equals 12,000 BTU per hour of cooling.

## How to Estimate AC Tonnage

**Method 1 — BTU conversion:**
```
Tons = Total BTU ÷ 12,000
```

**Method 2 — Square footage rule:**
```
Tons ≈ Sq ft ÷ 600 (moderate climate, 8-ft ceilings)
```

**Method 3 — Manual J:** The only method accepted for whole-home central AC sizing by code and most manufacturers.

For room-level tonnage, see **[AC Tonnage by Room Size](/blog/ac-tonnage-by-room-size)**. For BTU detail, see **[BTU per Square Foot](/blog/btu-per-square-foot-air-conditioning)**.

## Climate Adjustments

| Climate | Sq ft per ton |
|---|---|
| Hot/humid (South, Southwest) | 450 – 550 |
| Moderate (Midwest, Mid-Atlantic) | 550 – 650 |
| Cool (North, Pacific NW) | 650 – 800 |

**Figuring ac tonnage** without climate adjustment oversizes northern homes and undersizes southern ones.

## Tonnage vs Room Count

Open floor plans need fewer tons per sq ft than compartmentalized layouts because duct losses and internal walls add load. A 2,000 sq ft ranch and a 2,000 sq ft two-story with four bedrooms rarely need the same tonnage.

## FAQ

### How many tons of AC for 2,000 sq ft?

About 3 to 3.5 tons in moderate climate. Hot climates may need 4 tons; well-insulated cool-climate homes may be fine at 2.5 tons.

### What does tonnage of ac unit mean?

Cooling capacity: one ton = 12,000 BTU/hr. A 3-ton unit removes 36,000 BTU of heat per hour at rated conditions.

### Can I use an air conditioner ton calculator for window units?

Window units are rated in BTU, not tons. Divide BTU by 12,000 if you want to compare to central system sizing.

### Why do contractors size differently?

Some use rules of thumb; others run Manual J. Always ask for a load calculation worksheet on central AC quotes over 2 tons.
