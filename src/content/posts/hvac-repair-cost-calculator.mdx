An **hvac repair cost calculator** helps homeowners decide whether to fix or replace aging equipment. Related searches — **furnace repair cost calculator**, **duct cleaning cost calculator** — target specific repair types. Repair economics differ from replacement: the 50% rule says if repair exceeds half the replacement cost on a unit past 75% of its expected life, replace instead.

## Common HVAC Repair Costs

| Repair | Typical range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Service call / diagnostic | $75 – $200 | Often credited toward repair |
| Capacitor replacement | $150 – $400 | Most common AC failure |
| Contactor replacement | $150 – $350 | AC and heat pump |
| Refrigerant recharge (R-410A) | $200 – $600 | Find leak first — recharge alone is temporary |
| Blower motor | $400 – $1,500 | Depends on ECM vs PSC |
| Compressor replacement | $1,500 – $3,500 | Often triggers replace-vs-repair decision |
| Heat exchanger (furnace) | $1,500 – $3,500 | Safety issue — many units get replaced instead |
| Control board | $300 – $800 | Furnace or air handler |
| Thermostat (installed) | $150 – $400 | Smart stats at high end |

## Furnace Repair Cost Calculator Inputs

A **furnace repair cost calculator** should account for:

- Furnace age (15–20 year expected life for gas furnaces)
- Repair type (minor electrical vs major heat exchanger)
- Local labor rate ($75–$150/hr typical)
- Whether parts are still available for discontinued models

**Furnace repair cost calculator** output is most useful compared against **[Furnace Replacement Cost](/blog/furnace-replacement-cost)** — a $2,500 heat exchanger on a 18-year-old unit rarely makes sense.

## Duct Cleaning Cost Calculator

A **duct cleaning cost calculator** estimates $300–$700 for a typical single-family home. Factors:

| Factor | Cost impact |
|---|---|
| Home size | $25–$50 per vent is common pricing |
| Number of vents | 10–20 vents typical |
| Mold or asbestos | Requires remediation specialist — much higher |
| Dryer vent included | +$100–$150 |

EPA does not recommend routine duct cleaning unless you have visible mold, vermin infestation, or excessive debris. See NADCA guidelines before paying for unnecessary service.

## Repair vs Replace Decision

```
If repair cost > 50% of replacement cost AND unit age > 75% of expected life → replace
```

Example: $1,800 compressor on a 14-year-old 3-ton AC. Replacement runs $4,500–$6,500. Repair is 28–40% of replacement — borderline. At 16+ years, replace.

## FAQ

### How much does HVAC repair cost?

Most common repairs (capacitor, contactor, thermostat) run $150–$400 installed. Major repairs (compressor, heat exchanger) run $1,500–$3,500.

### Is a furnace repair cost calculator reliable?

It gives range benchmarks. Actual price depends on parts availability, labor market, and whether the unit is under warranty.

### How much does duct cleaning cost?

$300–$700 for a typical home with 12–15 vents. Larger homes or commercial systems cost more.

### When should I stop repairing and replace?

When repair exceeds 50% of replacement cost on equipment past 75% of expected life, or when the repair is a major component (compressor, heat exchanger) on a unit over 12–15 years old.
